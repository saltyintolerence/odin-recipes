Rice & Peas

Description

This Jamaican Rice and Peas is infused with coconut, Caribbean herbs and spices. Itâ€™s authentic and makes a flavourful side dish for family dinners.Jamaican rice and peas is a popular dish served at nearly every dinner table across the island.Traditionally, this recipe would take hours to make as Jamaicans sought to prepare nearly every ingredient from scratch. One such ingredient was fresh coconut milk.

Ingredients :

  • 1 tsbp veg oil
  • 6 scallions
  • 1 x 400g tin of gungo or dark red beans
  • 3 cloves garlic crushed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 x 400g tin coconut milk
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 1 uncut scotch bonnet

Method :

  1. Heat the oil in a your dutch pot.
  2. Add the diced scallions.
  3. Saute for 2 mins.
  4. Add the peas, beans, water, coconut milk, thyme and other seasonings and bring to the boil.
  5. Bring to the boil.
  6. Check the colour of the broth, it should be a light brown colour.
  7. Add the rice and the whole scotch bonnet.
  8. Bring all the ingredients to a boil and simmer.
  9. Cook the rice for 35 mins
  10. Serve with Jerk Chicken or other meat dishes
